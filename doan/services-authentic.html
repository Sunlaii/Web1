Authenticity Guarantee & partner sourcing

Our Promise of Authenticity

All products sold by The Nike Store are sourced from official manufacturers or authorized distributors. We perform quality checks and verification steps to ensure authenticity before items are shipped.

How we verify

We inspect branding, labeling, materials and packaging and match them with manufacturer standards. For select items we may include authenticity cards or serial checks when available.

Reporting suspicious items

If you believe an item you received is not authentic, contact our support team immediately with order details and photos. We will investigate and take appropriate action, including refund or replacement where applicable.
