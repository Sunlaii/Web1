Shipping Service — Terms & details

Overview

We provide reliable shipping for all orders. This page explains shipping options, estimated delivery times, tracking, and responsibilities.

Delivery Areas & Times

We ship domestically and to supported international regions. Delivery windows depend on your location and selected shipping method. Typical times:

  • Standard: 3–7 business days
  • Express: 1–3 business days
  • International: 5–15 business days (varies by country)

Shipping Costs & Promotions

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on weight, destination, and chosen carrier. From time to time we run promotions that may offer free or discounted shipping—these will be displayed at checkout.

Tracking & Delivery

All shipped orders include tracking numbers (where carrier supports it). Please use the tracking link provided in your order confirmation to follow the shipment.

Lost or Delayed Shipments

If your order is delayed beyond the expected window, contact customer support with your order ID and tracking number. We will coordinate with the carrier to investigate and resolve the issue.
