Refund Policy & Returns

Refund & Return Overview

We offer refunds or replacements for eligible products that arrive damaged, incorrect, or materially different from the listing. To start a refund, contact support with order details and photos.

Eligibility & Process

Refunds are considered when defects or mistakes are verified. We may request photos, order ID, and a short description. Once validated, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 3–14 business days depending on the provider.

Exceptions & Timelines

Personalized products, used items, or items outside the return window may not qualify for a refund. Please review order-specific timelines on the order confirmation email or contact support for clarification.
